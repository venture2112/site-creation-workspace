About VetCare

Our Story

Built on a Love for Animals

VetCare Veterinary Clinic was founded in 2009 by Dr. Sarah Mitchell with a simple mission: provide the kind of compassionate, thorough care she would want for her own pets. What started as a small two-exam-room practice has grown into a full-service veterinary hospital — but our heart has never changed.

Every pet who walks through our doors is treated with the same warmth, respect, and medical excellence. We believe in partnering with pet parents, not just treating symptoms. Education, communication, and trust are the foundation of every relationship we build.

Today, our team of 4 veterinarians and 12 support staff see over 1,200 active patients. We're proud to be an AAHA-accredited practice and a certified Cat-Friendly Practice.
