Comprehensive Pet Care Services

Wellness & Preventative Care

Prevention is the best medicine. Our comprehensive wellness programs are tailored to your pet's age, breed, and lifestyle — keeping them healthy and catching potential issues early. We partner with you to build a lifetime care plan that evolves as your pet grows.

  • Annual comprehensive physical exams
  • Custom vaccine protocols (core & lifestyle)
  • Nutritional counseling & weight management
  • Parasite prevention (fleas, ticks, heartworm)
  • Senior pet wellness & early disease screening
Starting at $85 for a comprehensive wellness exam

Surgical Services

Our state-of-the-art surgical suite is equipped with advanced monitoring technology to ensure the safest possible outcomes. From routine spay and neuter to complex orthopedic procedures, Dr. Rodriguez and our surgical team bring years of expertise and a gentle touch.

  • Spay & neuter (traditional & laparoscopic)
  • Soft tissue surgery (mass removal, exploratory)
  • Orthopedic surgery (cruciate repair, fracture repair)
  • Advanced monitoring & pain management
  • Pre-surgical bloodwork & consultation
Surgical estimates provided during consultation — call for details

Dentistry

Dental health is a critical part of your pet's overall well-being. Our comprehensive dental services — from routine cleanings to advanced periodontal therapy — help prevent pain, tooth loss, and systemic disease. We use digital dental X-rays to diagnose issues below the gumline.

  • Professional dental cleaning & polishing
  • Digital dental radiography
  • Tooth extractions & oral surgery
  • Periodontal disease treatment
  • At-home dental care coaching
Dental cleaning packages from $299 (includes anesthesia, monitoring, and X-rays)

Emergency & Critical Care

When every second counts, VetCare is here for you. Our emergency team is available 24/7 for urgent and life-threatening conditions. We maintain a fully stocked emergency pharmacy, in-house blood bank, and dedicated critical care unit to stabilize and treat your pet.

  • 24/7 emergency triage & stabilization
  • Trauma & accident care
  • Poisoning & toxin ingestion management
  • Respiratory distress & oxygen therapy
  • Intensive care unit with continuous monitoring
Emergency exam fee: $150 (additional charges based on treatment needed)

Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective treatment. Our in-house laboratory and advanced imaging capabilities allow us to get answers quickly — often while you wait. This means faster treatment decisions and less stress for you and your pet.

  • In-house bloodwork & urinalysis
  • Digital radiography (X-ray)
  • Abdominal & cardiac ultrasound
  • Cytology & microbiology
  • Allergy testing & thyroid panels
Diagnostic packages available — ask about our wellness lab bundles

Pharmacy & Records Portal

Convenience meets care. Our on-site pharmacy is stocked with a wide range of prescription medications, preventive treatments, and therapeutic diets. Plus, our secure online portal gives you 24/7 access to your pet's medical records, lab results, and prescription refill requests.

  • On-site pharmacy with common medications
  • Online prescription refill requests
  • Therapeutic diet & supplement orders
  • Secure medical records portal
  • Auto-refill & reminder notifications
Free home delivery on qualifying prescription orders over $50
