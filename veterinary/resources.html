Vaccination Schedule

Keeping your pet up-to-date on vaccinations is one of the most important things you can do to protect their health. Our veterinarians recommend the following schedule based on AAHA guidelines.

Vaccine Puppies Adults Seniors
Rabies 16 weeks Every 1-3 years Every 3 years
DHPP (Distemper) 6-16 weeks (3 doses) Every 1-3 years Every 3 years
Bordetella 8 weeks Every 6-12 months Every 12 months
Leptospirosis 12 weeks Annually Annually
Rabies (Feline) 12-16 weeks Every 1-3 years Every 3 years
FVRCP (Feline) 6-16 weeks (3 doses) Every 1-3 years Every 3 years

Schedule may vary based on your pet's lifestyle, breed, and health status. Consult with your veterinarian for a personalized plan.
