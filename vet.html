Our Services

Complete care for dogs, cats, and small pets — from routine checkups to advanced procedures.

🩺

Wellness Exams

Annual checkups, vaccinations, parasite prevention, and nutritional counseling.

🔬

Surgery

Spay/neuter, soft tissue, orthopedic, and dental surgery with full monitoring.

🦷

Dental Care

Cleanings, extractions, and oral health assessments under anesthesia.

🚨

Emergency Care

Same-day urgent visits for injuries, illness, and poisoning — call ahead.

🧬

Diagnostics

In-house lab, digital X-ray, ultrasound, and allergy testing.

💊

Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy with prescription diets, flea/tick prevention, and medications.
