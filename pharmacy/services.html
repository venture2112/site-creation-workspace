Our Pharmacy Services

Everything you need to live well — from custom compounds to free delivery, all with the personal touch of your neighborhood pharmacy.

Service 01

Compounding Pharmacy

When standard medications don't fit, we create solutions that do. Our board-certified compounding pharmacists formulate custom medications tailored to each patient's unique needs — whether it's an allergy-safe alternative, a more palatable flavor, or a precise non-standard dosage.

  • Custom dosage forms (lollipops, troches, transdermal gels, capsules)
  • Flavoring for pediatric and veterinary medications
  • Hormone replacement therapy (bioidentical HRT)
  • Pain management creams and gels
  • Allergy-safe alternatives (dye-free, gluten-free, sugar-free)
  • Veterinary compounding for your pets

Service 02

Immunizations

Stay protected year-round with our comprehensive immunization services. No appointment needed for most vaccines — just walk in during business hours. Our certified immunizing pharmacists will review your records and recommend the vaccines you need.

  • Seasonal flu shots (high-dose available for seniors 65+)
  • Shingles vaccine (Shingrix) — two-dose series
  • Pneumonia vaccines (Prevnar 20, Pneumovax 23)
  • Tdap (tetanus, diphtheria, pertussis)
  • Travel vaccines (hepatitis A & B, typhoid, yellow fever)
  • COVID-19 boosters (all major brands)
Walk In or Schedule
Service 03

DME & Medical Supplies

We carry a full range of durable medical equipment and daily living aids. Our team will help you choose the right product, handle the insurance paperwork, and set up delivery and installation — so you can focus on recovery.

  • Wheelchairs, walkers, rollators, and canes
  • CPAP machines, masks, and supplies
  • Diabetes testing supplies (glucometers, test strips, lancets)
  • Wound care supplies (bandages, dressings, tape)
  • Bath safety (shower chairs, grab bars, commodes)
  • Ostomy and incontinence products

Service 04

FREE Local Delivery

We believe transportation should never be a barrier to your health. That is why we offer completely free, same-day delivery within the city limits for all prescriptions and medical supplies. Our friendly drivers bring your medications right to your door.

  • Free same-day delivery within city limits
  • Medication synchronization — all refills ready on the same day
  • Automatic refill reminders (call, text, or email)
  • Discreet, insulated packaging for temperature-sensitive meds
  • Delivery available for DME and supplies as well
  • Contactless drop-off option available
