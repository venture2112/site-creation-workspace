Meet Your Pharmacist

Get to know the dedicated team behind Pharmacy+ — your neighbors, your advocates, your partners in health.

Dr. Sarah Mitchell, PharmD

Doctor of Pharmacy • Board-Certified Compounding Pharmacist
Owner & Lead Pharmacist — Over 15 Years Serving This Community

Dr. Sarah Mitchell founded Pharmacy+ in 2008 with a simple belief: that pharmacy care should be personal, not transactional. After spending nearly a decade working for large chain pharmacies, she saw firsthand how patients were being rushed through — their concerns unheard, their unique needs ignored.

She opened Pharmacy+ to change that. Today, she leads a team of three board-certified pharmacists and five dedicated support staff, all sharing the same commitment to individualized, compassionate care. Dr. Mitchell holds advanced certifications in sterile and non-sterile compounding, medication therapy management, and immunization delivery.

"Every patient who walks through our door has a story," she says. "Our job is to listen, to understand, and to create solutions that truly work for their life — not just their diagnosis."

Outside the pharmacy, Dr. Mitchell volunteers with local health fairs, serves on the board of the Community Health Alliance, and mentors pharmacy students from the nearby university. She lives in the neighborhood with her husband and two children.

Our Story

From Storefront to Community Cornerstone

What started as a single pharmacist's dream has grown into a trusted institution — but our values have never changed.

2008

Pharmacy+ Opens Its Doors

Dr. Sarah Mitchell leases a small storefront on Main Street and opens Pharmacy+ with one technician and a mission to redefine community pharmacy. First-year prescriptions: 3,200.

2011

Compounding Lab Established

Recognizing growing demand for custom medications, we invest in a state-licensed sterile and non-sterile compounding laboratory — one of only a handful of independent pharmacies in the region to offer this service.

2014

Expanded Clinical Services

We launch immunization services and medication therapy management (MTM), becoming a go-to resource for chronic disease management. Two additional pharmacists join the team.

2017

Free Delivery Program Launches

After hearing too many stories of patients skipping doses due to transportation barriers, we launch our free same-day delivery service — now serving over 1,200 households monthly.

2021

DME & Supply Center

We expand into durable medical equipment, creating a dedicated showroom and delivery service for wheelchairs, CPAP machines, and home health supplies.

2024

Still Growing, Still Independent

Today, Pharmacy+ serves over 8,000 patients, compounds 2,500+ custom preparations annually, and remains proudly independent — proof that community pharmacy is alive and thriving.
