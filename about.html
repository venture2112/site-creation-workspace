About Serenity Dental

Comfortable, expert care in the heart of Austin since 2010.

Our Practice

Serenity Dental was founded on a simple belief: going to the dentist shouldn't be something you dread. Dr. Priya Nair and Dr. Michael Rao created a practice where advanced dentistry meets genuine comfort — from the warm blankets and noise-cancelling headphones to the chairside manner that puts even the most anxious patients at ease.

With over 15 years of combined experience and thousands of happy smiles, we've built a reputation for exceptional cosmetic and general dentistry in a stress-free environment.

Meet Your Doctors

Experienced, compassionate, and committed to your comfort.

Dr. Priya Nair, DDS

Lead Cosmetic Dentist | 12 years experience

Dr. Nair is a graduate of UT Health San Antonio Dental School and completed advanced training in cosmetic and restorative dentistry at the Las Vegas Institute. She believes every patient deserves a smile they're excited to share with the world.

Dr. Michael Rao, DMD

General & Sedation Dentistry | 10 years experience

Dr. Rao earned his DMD from Tufts University and specializes in anxiety-free care and complex restorative cases. He has advanced certification in IV sedation and is known for his gentle touch.
