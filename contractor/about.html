Our Story

A Family-Owned Legacy of Commercial Service

Industrial Trades was founded in 1998 by master electrician James Crawford with a simple mission: deliver commercial-grade trades services with the integrity of a family business. What started as a two-man electrical shop has grown into a full-service mechanical contracting firm with over 50 employees serving 500+ commercial clients across the region.

Today, we're one of the most trusted names in commercial HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contracting. Our team includes NATE-certified HVAC technicians, licensed journeyman plumbers, and master electricians who share a common commitment to quality craftsmanship and safety excellence.

We're still family-owned and operated — and we still answer our own phones. When you call Industrial Trades, you talk to a real person who knows your facility and your systems.
