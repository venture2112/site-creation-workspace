Trade 01

Commercial HVAC

From single-zone rooftop units to complex central plant systems, our NATE-certified technicians deliver precision climate control solutions that keep your facility comfortable, efficient, and code-compliant year-round.

Commercial Installation

Design-build and plan-spec installation of rooftop units, split systems, VRF/VRV, heat pumps, and central plant equipment. We handle projects from 5 to 500+ tons.

Preventative Maintenance

Custom maintenance programs tailored to your equipment and budget. Monthly, quarterly, and annual inspections with priority scheduling and discounted repairs.

Emergency Repair

24/7/365 emergency HVAC service. Two-hour response time guaranteed for critical systems. Fully stocked service vehicles for same-day resolution.

Building Automation

BACnet, LonWorks, and Modbus-based BMS integration. Remote monitoring, scheduling, and optimization with real-time alerts and energy analytics.

Indoor Air Quality

IAQ assessments, UV-C germicidal lighting, HEPA filtration, MERV 13+ upgrades, humidification control, and CO2 monitoring for healthier indoor environments.

Chillers & Boilers

Centrifugal, screw, and scroll chillers. Fire-tube and water-tube boilers. Cooling towers, pumps, and hydronic system service and replacement.

Trade 02

Commercial Plumbing

Licensed commercial plumbers with deep experience in high-occupancy and high-demand plumbing systems. We design, install, and maintain the underground infrastructure that keeps your facility flowing.

Commercial Piping

Copper, PEX, CPVC, and cast iron piping systems. Hot and cold water distribution, domestic water risers, and recirculation systems for multi-story facilities.

Water Heaters & Boilers

Commercial tank and tankless water heaters, condensing boilers, indirect-fired storage tanks, and recirculation pump systems for high-demand applications.

Drain Cleaning

Hydro-jetting, auger snaking, video camera inspection, and trenchless pipe repair. We clear blockages and identify root causes in commercial drain systems.

Backflow Prevention

RPZ, double-check, and pressure vacuum breaker assembly installation, testing, and repair. Annual backflow certification and compliance with local codes.

Grease Traps & Interceptors

Hydromechanical and gravity grease trap installation, cleaning, and maintenance for restaurants, cafeterias, and food processing facilities. FOG program compliant.

Sewer Lines

Sanitary and storm sewer line installation, repair, and replacement. Trenchless pipe bursting, CIPP lining, and traditional excavation methods available.

Trade 03

Commercial Electrical

Licensed master electricians providing comprehensive electrical services from 120V to 480V three-phase. We design, build, and maintain the electrical backbone of commercial facilities.

Panel Upgrades & Distribution

Switchgear, panelboards, transformers, and distribution equipment. Load calculations, capacity upgrades, and 3-phase power installations for growing facilities.

Lighting Design & Retrofits

LED lighting design, retrofit, and controls. Occupancy sensing, daylight harvesting, and dimming systems. Energy code compliance and utility rebate support.

Emergency Generators

Natural gas, diesel, and propane standby generator systems. Automatic transfer switches, load banks, fuel systems, and monthly/weekly exercise testing.

Data Cabling & Infrastructure

Category 6A and fiber optic structured cabling. MDF/IDF build-outs, patch panels, cable management, and certification testing for data centers and offices.

Fire Alarm & Life Safety

Addressable fire alarm systems, smoke detection, mass notification, emergency lighting, and exit signage. NICET-certified technicians. AHJ-compliant designs.

Energy Audits & Efficiency

Power quality analysis, load profiling, energy consumption studies, and efficiency recommendations. We help you qualify for utility incentives and reduce operating costs.
